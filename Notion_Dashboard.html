⚠️ Risk Register
High Risk
Credit loss spike — Y1 cohort
First-cohort SMBs are underwriting unknowns. A 4% NCL rate would trigger product review and breach funding covenants.
High Risk
RBI regulatory change
Annual digital lending review could impose new fee disclosure rules or interest rate caps mid-launch.
Medium Risk
Platform integration delays
SAP Ariba sandbox access and Zoho Marketplace approval can take 6–10 weeks; any delay pushes Phase 2.
Medium Risk
Razorpay enters B2B BNPL
Razorpay Capital pivot to B2B BNPL would immediately threaten our distribution with their 500K merchant base.
Low Risk
SMB digital adoption lag
Some traditional SMBs may resist AA consent. WhatsApp-assisted KYB flow mitigates. Estimated 15% fallout.
Low Risk
Cost of funds increase
If RBI raises repo rate 50bps+, AIF credit facility cost rises. Hedged by moving to bank co-lending by Month 12.
